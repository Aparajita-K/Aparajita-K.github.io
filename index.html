Short Bio

I am currently a Visiting Scientist at Machine Intelligence Unit of Indian Statistical Institute. I completed my Ph. D in Computer Science from Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, India, in November, 2021 under the supervision of Professor Pradipta Maji . My research interests focus on Machine Learning and Bioinformatics with a particular emphasis on multi-view learning, subspace clustering, graph approximation, manifold learning, and multi-omics data integration. My dissertation is entitled Integrative Clustering of Multi-View Data: Subspace Clustering, Graph Approximation, to Manifold Learning. Link to my thesis
