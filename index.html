Aparajita Khan

Senior Research Fellow, Indian Statistical Institute

   

Short Bio

I am a Ph. D scholar at Machine Intelligence Unit of Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, India, since 2015. My research interests focus on Machine Learning and Bioinformatics with a particular emphasis on multi-view learning, subspace clustering, low-rank approximation, and multi-omics data integration. I am persuing my Ph. D under the supervision of Professor Pradipta Maji .
